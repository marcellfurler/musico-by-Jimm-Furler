Jesse Barrera & Albert Posis - The Hold On Tight Tour

Hari/Tanggal : Kamis, 23 May 2024

Waktu : 19.00 - 22.00 WIB

Lokasi : Aruba Hall, The Kasablanka, DKI Jakarta

Penyelenggara : Inside Indo

Deskripsi

Inside Indo & Livepass presents THE HOLD ON TIGHT TOUR!

Jesse Barrera and Albert Posis are two talented musicians who have carved out their own unique spaces in the global music industry.

Get ready to be swept away on an emotional journey with Jesse Barrera and Albert Posis on Hold On Tight Tour. Join them as they embark on a musical adventure filled with raw honesty, heartfelt melodies, and unforgettable moments. Let's hold on tight everyone! Because we promises this could be a concert you won't soon forget.

Look forward to singing with you guys on Thursday, 23rd May 2024. Get your tickets and prepare for an unforgettable night!
